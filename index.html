Privacy Policy for Basic Maths

Last Updated: February 2025

Thank you for using Basic Maths (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. No Data Collection

Basic Maths does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or location
  • The calculations you perform
  • Any input you enter into the app

All calculations are processed locally on your device and are never sent to any server or third party.

2. No Internet Permission

The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not request network permissions. There is no way for data to leave your device.

3. No Third-Party Services or Advertising

The App:

  • Contains no advertising
  • Uses no analytics or tracking frameworks
  • Does not integrate any third-party services that collect data

4. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, we do not have knowledge of any user's age, nor do we collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
