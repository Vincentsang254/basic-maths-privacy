Privacy Policy for Basic Maths

Last Updated: 14 December 2025

Thank you for using Basic Maths (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency.

1. No Data Collection

Basic Maths does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. This applies to all features of the app, including:

  • Standard calculator
  • Loan calculator
  • Discount calculator
  • Unit converter

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial data or loan details
  • Calculation inputs or results
  • Usage statistics or app activity
  • Device identifiers or location data

All calculations are performed locally on your device and never leave your phone.

2. Offline Functionality

Basic Maths works completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection and does not request network permissions.

3. Financial Calculations Disclaimer

The loan and discount calculators are provided for general informational and educational purposes only. Results are estimates and should not be considered professional financial advice.

4. No Third-Party Services

The App:

  • Contains no advertisements
  • Uses no analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not integrate third-party SDKs
  • Does not share data with any external services

5. Children's Privacy

Basic Maths is suitable for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 or any other age group.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app features change. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
